About Pst. David
Personal Profile

Born in Nigeria in September 1984, David Okonkwo is a Nigerian pastor and one of the most influential pastors in the world. David Okonkwo is the founder and Senir Pastor of St.Davids Ministries WorldWide, A.K.A. Chapel of Love with over 10,000 members, and its affiliated international churches called Chapel of Love International. Pastor David is known as Nigeria’s influential author of inspiring prosperity preaching.
