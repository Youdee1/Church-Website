Pastor David Okonkwo
Lead Pastor

Born in Nigeria in September 1984, David Okonkwo is a Nigerian pastor and one of the most influential pastors in the world. David Okonkwo is the founder and Senir Pastor of St.Davids Ministries WorldWide, A.K.A. Chapel of Love with over 10,000 members, and its affiliated international churches called Chapel of Love International. Pastor David is known as Nigeria’s influential author of inspiring prosperity preaching. He was once said he’d be surprised if he wasn’t at the level he is today from the depth of commitment heights of investments put into the Kingdom work. He is married to Assistant Pastor Thelma and they have 2 children.

History of St. Davids Ministries Worldwide

The young ministry had its inaugural Sunday morning service on the 1st of February, 1999 in his living room, then in the Ikeja area of Lagos state. As membership of the young but peculiar church grew into the hundreds and thousands, the surrounding grounds of his house became sitting area for the overspill from the living room. The ministry of the church and the person of Pastor David Okonkwo as a minister of the gospel became nationally recognized when the Lord Jesus Christ inspired the inauguration of the GRACE PROGRAMME and the television ministry, APOKALUPSIS. .The charity driven GRACE programme was instituted in 2005 and has become a major benevolence channel of the ministry on an annual basis. The objective of the programme as directed by the Lord Jesus is to extend the arm of care and sharing to the less privileged in the society through recognized non governmental organizations who deal directly with such people. In 2013 the KARIS AWARD, yet another inspiration by the Lord Jesus, which is aimed at appreciating Nigerians who have positively impacted the nation but have not been recognized by the government, was instituted and subsumed into the GRACE programme....
